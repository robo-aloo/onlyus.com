🔒 Your privacy

X25519 key exchange
Your keys are generated on your device each session. A shared secret is derived mathematically — it never travels anywhere.

XSalsa20-Poly1305 (libsodium secretbox)
Every message and every photo is encrypted before leaving your device. Each uses a unique random nonce — the gold standard.

WebRTC DTLS-SRTP
All video and voice is encrypted at the browser level. Not even the relay sees your face.

PeerJS relay
Sees two anonymous IDs connect — nothing else. After handshake it's peer-to-peer. No message data passes through it.

No storage
